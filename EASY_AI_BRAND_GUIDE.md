# Easy AI Brand Guide

## Company

Easy AI

## Audience

Small-business owners with roughly 10–50 employees, especially nontechnical owners who are interested in AI but concerned about complexity, trust, privacy, and losing control.

## Brand message

Easy AI makes artificial intelligence simple, practical, transparent, and controllable for small businesses.

## Slogan

“We do more, so your business can focus on what needs to be done.”

## Call to action

“Start your free business assessment today!”

## Creative direction

Use a photorealistic commercial feeling where possible in future asset phases. Focus on owners, employees, prospects, and customers in bright natural light. Keep the tone modern, trustworthy, human, and professional.

Avoid dashboards, robots, holograms, glowing AI brains, futuristic control rooms, and crowded technical graphics. AI should remain mostly invisible, with benefits shown through faster responses, timely follow-up, better customer service, and the owner remaining in control.

## Phase 1 wordmark

The wordmark is code-generated: a rounded gradient mark plus `EASY AI` text. No logo upload is required.
